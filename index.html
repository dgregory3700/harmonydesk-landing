Common questions

Who is this for?

Solo and small mediation practices that want their scheduling, documents, and billing in one place — without complexity.

How do clients book a session?

You share a private booking link. Clients request a session, the request lands in your inbox, and you approve or decline. Approvals send a confirmation and a calendar invite automatically.

Can I use my own court documents?

Yes. Upload your county court templates (.docx / .dotx). HarmonyDesk fills them from your case, session, and mediator details and gives you a ready-to-file document.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes. Explore the read-only demo, or start the 14-day free trial to use your own private workspace.
