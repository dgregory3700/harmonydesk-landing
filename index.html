Frequently asked questions

Does it sync with Google/Outlook calendar?

Not today. Sessions are tracked inside HarmonyDesk as an internal calendar for your mediation work.

Does it send reminders by SMS?

No. HarmonyDesk supports outbound email for invoices and messages; it does not provide SMS reminders.

Is it a booking link tool?

No. HarmonyDesk is not a booking links product. Sessions are entered and managed by the mediator.

What’s the core workflow?

Cases → Sessions → Invoices → Email sending → County reporting exports.
