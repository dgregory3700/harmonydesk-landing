Frequently asked questions

Do I need new software?

No. HarmonyDesk connects to tools you already use — your calendar, email, and (optionally) a phone/SMS line.

Is it compliant with confidentiality?

We design for confidentiality from the start. You choose what the assistant can access and where human review is required.

What about tone and empathy?

You set the voice guidelines. HarmonyDesk mirrors your preferred tone for intake, reminders, and follow‑ups.

Can I pause or limit actions?

Yes. You can require approval for certain tasks and set working hours and scope boundaries anytime.
